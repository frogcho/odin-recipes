Frog Cake

Happy frogday!

Original Recipe
Ingredients
  • Boxed french vanilla cake mix
  • Buttercream icing
  • Gel icing colour
    • Powdered sugar
  • Piping tips (of various sizes & shapes)
  • Piping bags
  • Candy eyeballs (optional!)
Instructions
  1. Bake cake according to the box's instructions
  2. Frost a layer of leaf-green icing onto the cake to form a base layer
  3. Separate remaining icing into 5 colours: green, pink, yellow, blue, and small amount of white (ensure you have black if you aren't using candy eyeballs)
  4. Pipe the base with desired piping tip
  5. Pipe mushrooms around the cylindrical surface area of the cake in various coloours
  6. Thicken buttercream icing with powdered sugar and form thick dollops for each frog body
  7. Use thin piping tip to form the arms and the legs
  8. Refrigerate as necessary to stabilise structural integrity of the icing frogs
  9. Detail the frogs' features
  10. Add in floral frosting details as desired
  11. Eat cake!
